American Family Insurance

Project Overview

  • Client: American Family Insurance
  • Role & Timeline: Senior UX Strategist, Aug 2022 – Present
  • Team & Stakeholders: 8+ designers & developers; Product owners, engineering leads, data analysts

Tools & Methods

  • Figma
  • Miro
  • Axure
  • User interviews
  • Usability testing
  • Design Thinking workshops

Background & Objectives

American Family Insurance sought to elevate its digital customer experience to:

  • Improve national JD Power satisfaction ranking (from #22 to top-tier)
  • Increase customer self-service and reduce reliance on call center
  • Standardize workflows across 21 sub-brands for consistent brand experience

Challenges

  • Legacy design debt: multiple siloed libraries slowed delivery and caused confusion.
  • Complex insurance jargon made product comparison difficult for customers.
  • Complex journey and blueprint across brands.

Approach & Process

  • Ran regular workshops to up-skill team
  • AI-driven innovation: Built Agentic AI personas to assist in discovery, documentation and a customer facing chat
  • Cross-functional “North Star” sessions: Aligned UX, Product, and Engineering on vision
  • Omni-channel design agents to assist with discovery and building empathy for our customers

Solutions

  • AI Chat Prototype: Demystified coverage options via conversational UI; Integrated with back-end rules engine for personalized quotes
  • Design/Token System: Single source of truth for 21 brands; Reduced implementation effort by 80%
  • Discovery Toolkit: Omni-channel design agents to assist with discovery and building empathy for our customers

Impact & Results

  • JD Power Satisfaction: Climbed from #22 to #1 within 12 months; maintained top rank for 3 years.
  • Self‑Service Uptake: +30% in chat-driven quote completions.
  • Design‑to‑Dev Handoff: 80% faster via token system.

Lessons Learned

  • Embedding AI early accelerates stakeholder buy-in, greater understanding of the problem and faster ideation
  • Regular cross-team rituals prevent scope creep and contribute to a group understanding of the problem we are trying to solve

Next Steps & Recommendations

  • Develop and scale AI chat for sales, policy servicing and claims
  • Enhance analytics to track conversational drop-off
  • Document case study visuals: flow diagrams, persona snapshots, before/after UI comps.
